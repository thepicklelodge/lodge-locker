How Sync Works: Lodge Locker queues changes and pushes them to CourtReserve via API. Each location has its own credentials. Product images pull from CourtReserve once connected.
Why can't I sync yet? Browser-based apps cannot directly call CourtReserve's API due to CORS security restrictions — CourtReserve's servers would need to whitelist your Netlify URL, which they don't do for arbitrary domains. A small backend proxy (one-day Supabase setup) solves this permanently. The 401 error you saw is actually good news — it means your URL and credentials are reaching CourtReserve's server correctly. The missing piece is the proxy.
